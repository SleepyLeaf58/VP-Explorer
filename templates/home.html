How to Play Scavenger Hunt

In the scavenger hunt, you will be directed to a room and given a riddle. In the room, there will be a hidden object with a code written upon it. Its location will be hinted through the riddle and you will have to find it! Once you've found the object, submit the code to reach the next item. Compete with other users to finish the hunt first!

How to Play Guessr

In this game, you will be shown a photo when you click "Show Photo". Click on the map to guess the location of the photo in the school. Click submit to see how close you got to the actual location!
